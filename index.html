Program Structure and Benefits

Ph.D. Program Highlights:

  • Duration: Typically 5-6 years with flexible timeline based on research progress
  • Funding: Full tuition coverage plus competitive stipend ($45,000/year) for all admitted students
  • Research Support: Annual research budget of $5,000 plus conference travel funding
  • Teaching Experience: Opportunities to serve as teaching assistant and develop pedagogical skills
  • Industry Connections: Internship opportunities with our partner companies including Google, Microsoft, and OpenAI
Our program emphasizes both theoretical rigor and practical application. You'll have the opportunity to publish in top-tier venues, present at international conferences, and collaborate with researchers from other leading institutions through our visiting scholar program.

Application Process and Next Steps

To complete your application, please ensure you submit:

  1. Online Application via our graduate admissions portal (deadline: December 15, 2024)
  2. Statement of Purpose (2-3 pages) detailing your research interests and career goals
  3. Three Letters of Recommendation from academic or research supervisors
  4. Official Transcripts from all institutions attended
  5. GRE Scores (optional but recommended for international applicants)
  6. Research Portfolio including publications, projects, and code samples
I strongly encourage you to reach out to potential faculty advisors before submitting your application. This will help you craft a more targeted statement of purpose and demonstrate your genuine interest in specific research areas. I would be happy to facilitate introductions to faculty members whose work aligns with your interests.
